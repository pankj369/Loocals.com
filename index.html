Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Loocals and how it works.

Loocals is a hyperlocal commerce platform. You enter your location, and we show you all verified local stores near you. You can browse their products, add items to your cart, and place an order — which gets delivered from the store directly to your home, fast.

Absolutely! That's exactly what Loocals is built for. Our platform uses your location to show you the nearest available stores. In most areas, you'll find stores within just 0.5 to 2 km of your home, ensuring fast and reliable delivery.

Because your products come from a store that's right in your neighborhood, delivery is blazing fast — typically within 15 to 30 minutes for most locations. We even offer live tracking so you can watch your delivery come to you in real-time.

Yes! We actively welcome local shop owners. Registering your store on Loocals is completely free. Once approved, your store will be visible to thousands of nearby shoppers. We provide a simple dashboard to manage your products, inventory, and orders.

We ensure prices on Loocals are fair and transparent. Store partners set their own prices, and we work closely with them to ensure pricing remains consistent with in-store rates. A small platform convenience fee may apply to some orders, which is always shown upfront before checkout.
